Our Offerings


Discover Wines from Around the World

At Wine:30 on Oak St, we celebrate wine in all its diversity. Our carefully curated selection spans the globe, featuring delicious wines produced right here in Texas alongside exceptional old world classics and exciting new world discoveries.

Whether you're seeking a familiar favorite or ready to explore something new, we offer all varietals at budget-friendly prices. From bold reds to crisp whites, elegant rosés to celebratory sparklings, there's something for every palate and occasion.

What sets us apart? We love introducing our customers to hidden gems—unique varietals and wines from lesser-known regions that you won't find everywhere else. Join our Wine Club to enjoy exclusive selections and expand your wine horizons, or stop by to let our knowledgeable staff guide you to your next great bottle.

Wine:30 on Oak St – Where the world of wine is always within reach.


Craft Beer

Enjoy a selection of craft beers for those who enjoy beer over wine.

At Wine 30, we believe that life's finest moments deserve to be savored with a drink that is equally exceptional. While our name may hint at our passion for fine wines, our love for craft beverages goes beyond the vine. In addition to our curated selection of exquisite wines, we take great pride in offering an impressive variety of craft beers that cater to every palate.


Delicious Bites

At Wine 30 on Oak Street, we complement our impressive selection of wines and craft beers with a delectable array of bites. From artisan cheese platters to mouthwatering charcuterie boards, our menu is designed to elevate your drinking experience. Indulge in gourmet small plates and tasty nibbles that pair perfectly with your favorite drink. Join us for a delightful culinary journey where each bite is as memorable as the sips. Cheers to good food and great company!


Live Music

Immerse yourself in the vibrant atmosphere of our Wine Bar with Live Music. Enjoy the soulful tunes, lively performances, and memorable moments as you savor our wines, craft beers and delectable bites. Cheers!
